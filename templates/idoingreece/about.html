The Person Behind the Platform

I am a fundraising events manager with 17 years of experience planning and delivering large-scale events for UK charities. I know how to manage logistics, work with suppliers, build timelines, and keep a complex event moving, calmly, professionally, and with attention to every detail. I also know what it means to be a bride planning a destination wedding from the UK. I know the late-night internet searches, the inbox full of vendor enquiries, the spreadsheets and the uncertainty and the moments of sheer excitement when something clicks into place. idoingreece.com brings both of those things together. The professional rigour of an experienced events manager. The personal insight of someone who has planned and lived the Greek wedding experience from the very first conversation to the very last dance

What We Believe
