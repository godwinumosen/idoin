The Wedding That Started Everything

I have worked in events for over 17 years, managing fundraising events for UK charities, coordinating complex programmes, working with suppliers and stakeholders under pressure. I know what goes into making an event extraordinary. I know the difference between a vendor who is merely competent and one who is genuinely passionate about what they do. Planning our wedding in Crete was, without question, the most joyful professional experience of my life. Our planner was exceptional. Our vendors, the florist, the photographer, the team who made every detail feel considered and personal, were extraordinary. What struck me most was not their skill, though that was undeniable. It was their passion. Every single person we worked with cared deeply about our wedding. They were invested in it. They wanted it to be as beautiful and meaningful for us as we did.
