Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Effective Date: 3 March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users of idoingreece.com.

I Do In Greece is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use and store it.

1. Who We Are

I Do In Greece operates the website idoingreece.com, a directory connecting international couples planning weddings in Greece with wedding professionals (“Vendors”) operating across Greece.

  • Website: idoingreece.com
  • Email: hello@idoingreece.com

This policy explains your rights and our obligations under applicable data protection laws including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us
  • Your name and email address
  • Wedding date or location preferences
  • Planning details you voluntarily share
  • Any information included in messages or enquiries
Information collected automatically
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Referring website or search term
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

3. Information from Vendors

If you apply to list your business on idoingreece.com we may collect:

  • Your name and business name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Business location and category
  • Social media profiles
  • Portfolio images and business descriptions
  • Payment information (processed via secure third-party providers)

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to enquiries and provide support
  • Send newsletters or updates where you have consented
  • Process vendor applications and manage listings
  • Improve the website through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security of the platform

We never sell personal data and do not share information with advertisers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent – when you subscribe to newsletters or waitlists
  • Contract – when processing is required to provide services
  • Legitimate Interests – improving our platform and analytics
  • Legal Obligation – where processing is required by law

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Enquiry data: up to 2 years after last contact
  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe
  • Vendor listing data: duration of listing and up to 3 years after termination
  • Analytics data: anonymised and retained up to 26 months

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience.

  • Essential cookies – required for site functionality
  • Analytics cookies – understand website usage
  • Preference cookies – remember settings and preferences

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict how we process your data
  • Receive your data in portable format
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures including secure hosting, SSL encryption and restricted access controls.

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes occur, the effective date at the top of the document will be updated.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: hello@idoingreece.com

We aim to respond to privacy enquiries within 5 working days.
